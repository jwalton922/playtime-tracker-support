Playtime Tracker

Sports Team Time Management App

Welcome to Playtime Tracker Support

Playtime Tracker helps youth sports coaches manage player time on the field, ensuring fair play time for all team members. Track playing time, manage substitutions, and keep detailed records of each game.

Key Features

  • Real-time player time tracking
  • Easy tap-to-substitute interface
  • Customizable field/court terminology for different sports
  • Period/quarter/inning management
  • Playing time statistics and percentages
  • Player profiles with jersey numbers and positions
  • Game history and records

  • Make sure you're using the latest version of the app
  • Try restarting the app to see if the problem persists

Common Issues & Solutions

Timer not starting

Make sure you have players assigned to the field before starting the game. Tap on players in the bench section to move them to the field.

Players not moving between field and bench

The game may need to be paused first. Try pausing the game, then tapping players to move them.

App crashes on startup

Try deleting and reinstalling the app. Your data is saved and will be restored.
